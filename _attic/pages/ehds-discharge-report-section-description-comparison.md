# EHDS Discharge Report Section Description Comparison

| Section in EHDSDischargeReport logical model | Description from logical model | Section in CompositionEuHdr FHIR profile | Description from FHIR profile |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.alerts` - Section: Alerts | Information about substantial alerts or warnings, including allergies, that health professionals should be aware of. | `section[sectionAlert]` - Alert Section | Information about substantial alerts or warnings, including allergies, that health professionals should be aware of. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.encounterInformation` - Section: Encounter information | Section: Encounter information. | No dedicated active Composition section. The encounter is represented by `Composition.encounter`; an `sectionEncounters` slice exists only in commented-out FSH. | N/A |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.admissionEvaluation` - Section: Admission evaluation | Admission evaluation section should be reported only if it is relevant to ensure continuity of care. | `section[sectionAdmissionEvaluation]` - Hospital Admission evaluation | Hospital Admission evaluation. Admission evaluation should be reported only exceptionally, if it is relevant to ensure continuity of care. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.admissionEvaluation.objectiveFindings` | Objective findings, such as anthropometric measurements, vital signs, or objective anatomical findings of physical examination. | `section[sectionVitalSigns]` - Vital signs; `section[sectionPhysicalFindings]` - Physical findings | Vital signs includes blood pressure, body temperature, heart rate, respiratory rate, and may include other clinical findings such as height, weight, body mass index, head circumference, pulse oximetry, and relevant trends. Physical findings: Physical findings. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.admissionEvaluation.functionalStatus[x]` | An individual's ability to perform normal daily activities required to meet basic needs, fulfil usual roles, and maintain health and well-being. | `section[sectionFunctionalStatus]` - Functional status | Narrative description of the patient's capability to perform acts of daily living, including possible needs for continuous assessment by third parties. Future versions will specify profiles to express disabilities and functional assessments. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.patientHistory` - Section: Patient health history | Patient health history, anamnesis, containing only information that is highly relevant for this specific episode of care. The model expects that a patient summary is available. | `section[sectionPatientHx]` - Hx general Reported Section | May provide synthetic anamnesis, such as chronological summary of phases of the pathology clustered by clinical information, and anecdotal evidence collected from the patient in narrative form. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.courseOfEncounter` - Course of inpatient or outpatient encounter | Course of inpatient or outpatient encounter. | `section[sectionHospitalCourse]` - Hospital course | Hospital course describes the sequence of events from admission to discharge in a hospital facility. It includes basic encounter information, diagnostic summary, pharmacotherapy, major procedures, medical devices, significant findings during hospital stay, and clinical synthesis. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.courseOfEncounter.diagnosticSummary` | Problems treated or affecting care during the encounter. At least one treated problem should be marked. Other problems are recorded only if important for continuity of care after discharge. | `section[sectionDiagnosticSummary]` - Problem specification in narrative form | Problems or diagnoses affecting care during the inpatient case or important for continuity of care. The section differentiates treated and untreated problems, and may classify problems as present on admission, acquired during hospital stay, or not applicable. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.courseOfEncounter.procedures` | Procedures performed during the encounter that are significant for continuity of care, such as surgery, instrumental interventions, chemotherapy, radiotherapy, dialysis or circulation support. Diagnostic procedures are typically captured as observation results and not repeated here. | `section[sectionSignificantProcedures]` - Significant procedures | Significant surgical and non-surgical procedures performed during hospitalisation that are important for continuity of care. Purely diagnostic procedures are excluded; if none were performed, this should be explicitly stated using IPS absent/unknown data. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.courseOfEncounter.medicalDevicesAndImplants` | Medical devices, including implants, whose use was started, stopped, or otherwise modified during the encounter. | `section[sectionMedicalDevices]` - Medical devices and implants | Implants and used medical devices that affected or may affect health service provision, including devices explanted or stopped during hospitalisation. If blank, the reason should be explicitly stated using IPS absent/unknown data. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.courseOfEncounter.pharmacotherapy` | Significant medication treatments during the encounter. The content may be repeated in the medication summary for a full overview of the patient's medications. | `section[sectionPharmacotherapy]` - Pharmacotherapy | Selected drug treatment during hospitalisation, including medicinal products administered during hospitalisation and discontinued before discharge when important for continuity of care. Products continuing after discharge are also recorded in the medication summary. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.courseOfEncounter.objectiveFindings` | Objective findings, such as anthropometric measurements, vital signs, or objective anatomical findings of physical examination. The line between objective findings and test results is not clear and is left to implementers. | Partial match: `section[sectionSignificantResults]`; `section[sectionVitalSigns]`; `section[sectionPhysicalFindings]`. No course-specific objective-findings section is defined. | Significant results covers significant medical test results of functional, diagnostic, laboratory, and imaging examinations performed during encounter, including pending orders. Vital signs and physical findings cover specific observation categories. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.courseOfEncounter.testResults` | Significant medical test results of functional, diagnostic, laboratory, and imaging examinations performed during encounter. This may include orders whose results have not yet arrived. | `section[sectionSignificantResults]` - Significant medical test results | Significant medical test results of functional, diagnostic, laboratory, and imaging examinations performed during encounter. This may include orders whose results have not yet arrived. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.synthesis` - Clinical synthesis | Clinical synthesis clustered by managed conditions, such as description of reasons and course of encounter. It may include clinical reasoning in clinically complex conditions. | `section[sectionSynthesis]` - Synthesis | Clinical synthesis, such as description of reasons and course of hospital stay, clustered by managed conditions. It may include clinical reasoning in clinically complex conditions. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.dischargeDetails` - Discharge details | Structured information should be provided; if not available, at least a section narrative should be present. | `section[sectionDischargeDetails]` - Discharge details | The hospital discharge status or disposition of the patient having a hospitalization. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.dischargeDetails.objectiveFindings` | Objective findings, such as anthropometric measurements, vital signs, or objective anatomical findings of physical examination. | No discharge-specific objective-findings section. These may be represented in `section[sectionDischargeDetails]` narrative or in observation-oriented sections such as `section[sectionVitalSigns]`, `section[sectionPhysicalFindings]`, or `section[sectionSignificantResults]`. | N/A for a discharge-specific section. Related profile descriptions are discharge details, vital signs, physical findings, and significant medical test results. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.dischargeDetails.functionalStatus[x]` | An individual's ability to perform normal daily activities required to meet basic needs, fulfil usual roles, and maintain health and well-being. | `section[sectionFunctionalStatus]` - Functional status. The FHIR profile does not define a discharge-specific functional-status slice. | Narrative description of the patient's capability to perform acts of daily living, including possible needs for continuous assessment by third parties. Future versions will specify profiles to express disabilities and functional assessments. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.medicationSummary` - Medication recommended after discharge | Summary information on medication recommended after discharge, indicating whether medication is changed or newly started. The overview also includes discontinued medication. | `section[sectionDischargeMedications]` - Hospital discharge medications | Hospital discharge medications defines medications the patient is intended to take, or stop, after discharge. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.medicationSummary.medicationUse` | Details about medication and dosage. | `section[sectionDischargeMedications].entry` - references `MedicationRequestEuHdr`, `MedicationDispenseEuHdr`, or `MedicationStatementEuCore` | Entries represent the medications the patient is intended to take, or stop, after discharge. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.carePlan` - Care plan and other recommendations after discharge | Care plan and other recommendations after discharge. | `section[sectionPlanOfCare]` - Plan of Care Section | Narrative description of expectations for care, including proposals, goals, and order requests for monitoring, tracking, or improving the patient's condition. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.body.carePlan.otherRecommendations` | Other recommendations or advice after discharge, such as recommendation to suggest hip replacement, reduce cigarettes, stop smoking, or increase physical exercise. | Partial match: `section[sectionPlanOfCare]` narrative and/or `section[sectionDischargeInstructions]` - Hospital Discharge Instructions | Plan of care covers expectations for care, proposals, goals, and order requests. Hospital discharge instructions covers hospital discharge instructions. |
| `EHDSDischargeReport.attachments` - Report attachments | Report attachments. Implementations may define allowed attachment types and may ignore unexpected attachments for security reasons. | `section[sectionAttachments]` - Library of attachments | Lists documents and attachments associated to this report. |
| No direct top-level section in logical model | N/A | `section[sectionAllergies]` - Allergies and Intolerances Section | Documents relevant allergies or intolerances for the patient, including reaction, causative agents when possible, criticality, and certainty. It should also clearly state when no allergy information is available or no allergies are known. |
| No direct top-level section in logical model | N/A | `section[sectionProblems]` - Problem List Section | Lists and describes clinical problems or conditions currently being monitored for the patient. It also supports documenting that no problem information is available or no relevant problems are known. |
| No direct top-level section in logical model | N/A | `section[sectionImmunizations]` - Immunizations Section | Defines the patient's current immunization status and pertinent immunization history. It may contain the immunization history relevant to the summarized period and supports documenting no information or no known immunizations. |
| No direct top-level section in logical model | N/A | `section[sectionDischargeInstructions]` - Hospital Discharge Instructions | Hospital Discharge Instructions. |
